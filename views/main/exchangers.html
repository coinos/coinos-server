Exchangers

Our goal is to establish a network of trusted individuals who can provide a consistent experience and reliable price for people wanting to trade bitcoins in Vancouver.

Join Us!

Meeting Procedure

  • Contact one of us to let us know your availability
  • Tell us how much you want to buy and provide us with an address to send to
  • We'll arrange a time to meet at a public place like a café or a bank
  • When we meet, we'll look up the exchange rate
  • We send the bitcoins to your address using our phone or laptop
  • We'll wait to confirm the transaction at blockchain.info
